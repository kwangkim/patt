Pros on a cloud

  • Simple

    • We only need a browser.
    • Same environment: in class or home

  • Synced

    • Up-to-date. No flash-drive.
    • Revision. History.

  • Cheap

    • Free or cheap cloud systems
    • Easy maintenance: No OS/program installation/updates

  • Online education friendly.

Cons on a cloud

  • Slower or weaker

    • Sometimes slower than desktop programs.
    • May not provide full functionaries.

  • Unstable

    • Internet is necessary.
    • Some free servers are not stable.

  • Not fully mobile friendly

    • Sometimes does not work well with mobile devices.
    • But it is getting better.
